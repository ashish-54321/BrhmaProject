Brahmanand Jangid

A social reformer, educational visionary and a great philanthropist, Brahmanand Jangid is the light that is guiding us through the way forward. While he is no more with us, his rich legacy is being carried forward by everyone connected with Jangra saba family.

What is Jangra Sabha

Welcome to Jangra Sabha, a dedicated platform designed to bring together and celebrate the Jangid Brahmin community in Faridabad. Our website serves as a comprehensive database, providing detailed information about approximately 500 Jangid Brahmin families in the region. We strive to ensure that the family details are kept accurate and up-to-date, offering a reliable resource for community members to connect, share, and stay informed. Whether you're looking for family histories, contact information, or other essential updates, Jangra Sabha is here to help foster a sense of unity and belonging within our community. Stay connected with us as we continue to grow and strengthen our shared heritage!
